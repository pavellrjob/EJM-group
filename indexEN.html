How to choose materials for repair?

Adhesion and compressive strength are important properties in almost all repair and protection work.

The strength of the base material and the repair materials should be approximately the same.

However, if the strength of both the base and repair materials are high,the repair material would fail prematurely due to an uneven flow of stress and strain.
