How do we work

We have a step-by-step plan for the construction of your house, which will give you confidence in your security and in our professionality.

1. Having a conversation with you to begin with

Prior to the commencement of the project, there will be a meeting between you and our specialists to talk about your ideas and the volume of the construction works. Which are your preferences? Which kind of style do you prefer? Which aspects are important to you and shall be considered? We are happy if you are. Hence it is always an advantage to make an exact plan for your wishes and your ideas, to be able to meet them.

2. Presentation of the model house

Following the discussion will be developed a house plan, and the model house will be presented. If there are certain things that you don't like, it is understood that we will adapt these things according to your wishes.

3. Planning and calculation

The finishing of the model house, the creation of drawings and the complete calculation of all construction processes will be carried out.

4. Presentation of the building project

With this step we present to you your building project in pictures. We will talk about all construction stages and set deadlines for each stage

5. Conclusion of a contract for the construction of the site

This step includes the signing of the contract, the coordination of all construction stages, the method of payment, performance conditions for each construction stage, at which, if you wish, you can always have a look.

6. Placement of the construction order

7. Waiting for the construction permit

8. Commencement of the construction

This step primarily includes the preparation of the construction site. We make place for all construction materials, a storage site, a transport access etc.

9. Construction stages

Step 1: Filling of the foundation

Concrete foundations are an important element of the construction works. Depending on the type, the foundation should stand for some time and get more stability.

Step 2 Construction of the house basement

The base is an extension of the foundation. However, it is rather susceptible to negative environmental effects (rain, snow, fluctuations of temperature usw.)

Step 3 Construction of the house walls

Exterior und interior walls are being constructed simultaneously, in order to spread the load on the foundation. The construction of the walls is planned in a way to make sure that it is completed during the season. When constructing a two-storey-building, the application of the floor tiles has an influence on the working speed.

Step 4: Roofer works

According to the pace in which the project developes and depending on the chosen material, the installation of the spar system of the roof, of the house the installation of the roof material will be carried out.

Step 5: Integration of windows and outer doors

Step 6: Insulation of the house

Step 7: Technical communication in the house

Interior works are only possible, when the house ist protected reliably from exterior influences, like for example rain, snow, wind.

Step 8: The arrangement of the cobblestones around the house

The flooring will be done a year after the house was constructed. After this time has elapsed, the flooring will be a real enrichment for the house. Underneath the cobble stones, there can be created a rain canal system or a drainage system.

Step 9: Final works

The final works can be carried out in each time of the year, even if the house is inhabited. Due to changes in the weather this could last a long time.

10: Renting/Selling of the house

We hand-over to you the completed house. There will be a hand-over of keys and documents.

Project Payment

The payment is effected in several steps and for your security will be dealt with in the contract and recorded. Each payment will be dealt with separately. The purchaser can at any time come to the construction site and inspect it. Prior to this, he will get in advance a construction plan with the appropriate construction stages and the construction conditions for each construction stage, to give him a full overview over the project at any time.
